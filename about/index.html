About

I am Doctoral student in the Department Department of Computational Mathematics, Science and Engineering - CMSE at Michigan State University advised by Dr. Jose A. Perea. Before MSU, I completed my MSc in Mathematics and BS in Mathematics at Los Andes University in Bogota, Colombia. There I was advised by Dr.Andrés Angel and Dr. Jose A. Perea. My master’s dissertation is titled Estimations on persistent homology.
